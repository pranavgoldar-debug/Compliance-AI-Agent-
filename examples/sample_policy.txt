ACME CORP — INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY (v3.2)

Section 4. Access Control

4.1 All employee access to production systems must be provisioned through the
identity provider (Okta). Direct local accounts on production hosts are
prohibited except for break-glass accounts, which must be stored in the
corporate password vault and rotated within 24 hours of any use.

4.2 Access reviews must be conducted at least once per calendar quarter. The
system owner is responsible for attesting to the appropriateness of each
user's access. Reviews must be retained for a minimum of three years.

4.3 Multi-factor authentication is required for all access to systems
processing customer data. SMS-based MFA is not permitted; only TOTP or
WebAuthn factors are acceptable.

Section 5. Data Retention

5.1 Customer personal data must be deleted within 30 days of account closure,
except where retention is required by law (e.g. tax records, which are
retained for seven years).

5.2 Backups containing personal data must be encrypted at rest using AES-256
or stronger and stored in a region that complies with the customer's
contractual data residency requirements.

Section 6. Incident Response

6.1 Security incidents involving suspected unauthorized access to customer
data must be reported to the Security team within 1 hour of detection and
to affected customers within 72 hours of confirmation.

6.2 A post-incident review must be conducted within 10 business days of
incident closure. Findings and remediation owners are tracked in the
incident management system.
